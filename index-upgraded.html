Questions

Frequently Asked

How do pilots work?
We start with a short intake to understand your needs, align on scope and risk tolerance, then deliver a focused pilot with clear, measurable outcomes within 2-3 weeks.
Do you store personal data?
Minimal collection, zero resale. All data is encrypted by default and reports are fully anonymized. We design for GDPR and CCPA compliance from day one.
Where are you active?
Active pilots in Greece and Rwanda, expanding across Africa and Europe through partnerships. We are remote-first and serve clients globally.
What does it cost?
Transparent, outcome-based pricing. Pilots start at €2,500. Full services scale with your organization. Education sector discounts available.
How do I get started?
Reach out via email or WhatsApp with your organization's needs. We'll schedule a 30-min call to explore if Cobalt Shield is a good fit.
