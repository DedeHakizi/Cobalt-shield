Questions?

Frequently Asked

How do you ensure data privacy?
We employ end-to-end encryption, zero-knowledge architecture, and comply with international privacy standards. All systems are regularly audited by independent security researchers.
What regions do you serve?
We primarily focus on Africa and emerging markets across Asia and Latin America. However, our open-source tools are deployed globally by thousands of communities.
Can I use your tools offline?
Yes! All our core tools are designed to work offline-first. Synchronization happens when connectivity is available, making them ideal for unreliable network conditions.
How much does it cost?
Our open-source tools are free. We offer enterprise support and custom solutions on a case-by-case basis. Community organizations often qualify for special pricing.
Do you offer training?
Absolutely. We provide comprehensive training programs, workshops, and documentation. We also work directly with organizations to build security capacity.
